How to install mods

  1. Ensure that the developer has updated their mod(s) for the latest patch
  2. Follow their instructions to download the mod file(s)
  3. If a downloaded file ends with .zip, unzip it
  4. Place the unzipped file(s) in your Documents > Electronic Arts > The Sims 4 > Mods directory
  5. Ensure that "Enable Custom Content and Mods" is checked under Game Options > Other
  6. Reload your game, and check that the mod has successfully installed

NOTE: Mod files may end in either .package or .ts4script
